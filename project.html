PROJECT:FORMS

Project:FORMS

Project:FORMS is a Friendly Online Request Management System.

Project:FORMS is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline your academic administrative tasks. Here, you can easily request enrollment forms and other essential educational documents required for your enrollment process. Additionally, the website allows you to check your grades and access a copy of your certificate of enrollment, providing a one-stop solution for all your academic needs.

Project:FORMS provides a user-friendly platform to manage all your academic requirements efficiently and conveniently.

LIBRARY SYSTEM

Library System

The Library System website provides an efficient platform for users to borrow books, where they can easily log into library rooms using a QR code and check detailed availability information such as current stock levels and the maximum number of books they can borrow at any given time. This streamlined process ensures a user-friendly experience for managing library resources and accessing educational materials.
